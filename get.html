Get Sol

When you want to get Sol Energy you will have to fill out the form on this page. By being a part of this project, you will help the environment a lot, you will be part of getting rid of coal and gas as a power-source. It will also help decreasing the amount of windmills, which are bad for birds, or building large scale solar parks which take up huge landsites. Click to learn more.

When filling out the form it’s a few things that can be nice to be aware of. You have the option to not be a part of the investment, but you can also be one of the investors if you want. Either way you will have the option to buy back parts of the solar array by making deals with the investors through our website.

After you fill out the form below, we will contact you and make a contract before your project goes live. When the project goes live, the possible investors will see your project and might begin putting down an investment. When the project is fully funded we will be begin the installation of the solar array.
