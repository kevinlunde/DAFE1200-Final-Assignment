Invest

Here you can invest in solar panels for others electricity. You buy the panel and sell the electricity it generates to the client either it be a private home or a corporate office building. We at Sol Invest provide different projects for you as an investor and give you the information you need, so you can choose which client to cooperate with.

This will both help the environment with clean, green power and turn into an long-term investment for you as there is no set time for the panels to be removed.

When 'Investing Sol' you buy solar panels which will be placed in a client-provided area, and sell the electricity. They are to me maintained by the client and require no more than the initial start-up payment. You will get access to see how much electricity your panels produce, and you can sell or give away solar panels to other Sol investors. Click to learn more.
